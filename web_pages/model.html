Limitations

  • Our complete dataset has the better predictive score, however, we found it is actually not predicting storms on days with conditions where we know there are storms.
  • We found just the storm data (the smaller dataset) had a lower predictive score, but predicted storms more accurately.
  • We had a lot of data from the buoys, but there have not been enough storms in the last 15 years to account for all of the buoy data we had.
