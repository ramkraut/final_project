Project Overview

Rick Gentile, Ruthy Amkraut, Megan Moroney

Our project includes fifteen years of buoy data utilized in a Machine Learning predictive model for when Hurricane and Tropical Storms will occur in the Gulf of Mexico.

Our data includes fifteen years of bouy data and fifteen years of hurricane and tropical storm data. With this data, our goal was to create a predictive model using machine learning in order to predict not only whether or not there would be a storm, but to predict the severity of that storm (tropical storm vs. category of hurricane).
